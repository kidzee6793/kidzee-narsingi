About Us

KIDZEE Preschool, Narsingi is committed to nurturing every child's potential through a joyful, safe and engaging learning environment. Our unique Five Minds approach develops:

  • Focused Mind
  • Inventive Mind
  • Analytical Mind
  • Empathetic Mind
  • Conscientious Mind

We believe children learn best through play, exploration, creativity, movement and meaningful experiences.
